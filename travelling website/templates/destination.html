Explore Our Top Destinations

From Himalayan peaks to Goan beaches — find your next adventure.

  • Ladakh

    High-altitude deserts, Buddhist monasteries, and breathtaking mountain passes.Ladakh is a high-altitude region in the Kashmir area of the Indian subcontinent, divided between India, Pakistan, and China

  • Kerala

    Kerala, India, is a tropical paradise known for its lush green landscapes, backwaters, and beautiful beaches. Located on the southwestern coast of India, it offers a unique cultural experience with its ancient temples, traditional dance forms, and flavorful cuisine.

  • Jaipur

    Jaipur is a regal paradise of culture and legacy, rich with architectural jewels, and is affectionately dubbed "The Pink City" for the pink color of its old structures. City Palace provides you a sense of the opulent lifestyle enjoyed by Jaipur's royal family.

  • Goa

    Goa, India, is a tropical paradise known for its stunning beaches, rich culture, and lively nightlife. The state is a perfect blend of Portuguese and Indian cultures with its historic landmarks, mouth-watering cuisine, and exciting water sports

  • Varanasi

    Varanasi is a city on the Ganges river in northern India that has a central place in the traditions of pilgrimage, death, and mourning in the Hindu world.

  • Manali

    A Himalayan haven for snow lovers, trekkers, and serene mountain vibes.A gift of the Himalayas to the world, Manali is a beautiful township nestled in the picturesque Beas River valley

  • Andaman Islands

    Pristine beaches, coral reefs, and water adventures in a tropical paradise.The Andaman and Nicobar Islands, a union territory of India, comprise 836 islands, of which only 31 are inhabited.

  • Agra

    Agra Fort is a historical Mughal fort in Agra, India, where several rulers were crowned and imprisoned. It was built by Akbar in red sandstone and later renovated by Shah Jahan in white marble.

  • Meghalaya

    Cloud-kissed hills, living root bridges, and waterfalls in India's northeast gem.Meghalaya is a state in northeast India, formed in 1972 by carving out districts from Assam.

  • Udaipur

    Known as the City of Lakes — royal palaces, boat rides, and romantic charm.

  • Munnar

    Misty hills, tea plantations, and serene escapes in the heart of Kerala’s Western Ghats.
